Express Yourself

  • What are your honest feelings about the day… Would you repeat it? What would you change?
  • How are you feeling about tomorrow?
  • What is one thing you’re grateful for right now?
  • Did you do anything that brought you personal joy?
  • Did anyone contribute to your day and a negative way or a positive way?
  • Do you feel like your emotions are valid? Remind her no one can tell you how to feel.
  • Did you apologize for something that you didn’t do and wasn’t wrong?
  • Are you confident in the person you were today? Or are you fatigued from putting on a façade?
  • On a scale of 1 to 10, how content are you?
